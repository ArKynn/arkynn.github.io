Hi! I'm David Mendes!

I'm a game developer, focused on gameplay and game systems. I'm proficient in C# and have experience with python, while learning C++ on the side. I've graduated from UHLT with a bachelor's degree in "Videojogos".

I've always loved videogames, and found a new passion when I first wondered how games were made. I've developed several games using Unity Engine, as well as smaller prototypes and physical media. I mainly love to develop new gameplay features and mechanics from the ground up, while fully enjoying seeing my code giving life to any other aspects of a game.

My projects

Out of Sight

Out of Sight is a first-person, singleplayer, stealth-focused heist game. Your goal is to complete various missions without alerting anyone, otherwise you'll have to evade the authorities.

Visit game page

PodScape

PodScape is a Escape Room puzzle game, where you take control of a spaceship crash survivor which has to repair various systems inside a destroyed lifepod in order to open its doors and escape.

Visit game page

Pizz'Ahoy

Pizz'Ahoy is a VR cooking simulator, where you play as a pirate aiming to achieve his italian cooking dreams. Receive, prepare, and deliver various pizza orders aboard a pirate ship, and maintain a stable income to pay rent to your captain.

Visit game page

Deadman Crawling

Deadman Crawling is a horror game, where you play as a fisherman that survived a monster attack by fleeing into a cave. Escape from the monster while trying to find your missing friends.
